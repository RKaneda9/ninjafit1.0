Central Florida's First Multi-Sport Fitness Competition for Weight Lifters, Ninja Warriors and Obstacle Course Racers!

On {{eventDateFormatted}}, NinjaFit Gym, and presenting sponsor Invel, is bringing you the fitness event of the winter!

Bring The Ruckus is the first team obstacle challenge where Ninja Warrior meets Obstacle Course Racing meets Weight Lifting in one thrilling event.

Every competitor will be pushed to the limit and no matter your skill level you will be put on display like never before.

Teams of 3 will compete in relay race style competition to see who can actually “Bring The Ruckus” in Central Florida. Each team must consist of at least 1 female and 1 male athlete.

The competition will take place at NinjaFit Gym, Orlando’s only OCR and Ninja Warrior training facility where your team may have to conquer the Warped Wall after a set of soul crushing thrusters and rope climbs, or fling across the monkey bars after conquering the battering ram, running up and down Goliath and attempting the salmon ladder.

Each team will choose which of their teammates will conquer which part of the course to see who can get the most points and finish in the fastest time.

Every team will have an opportunity to conquer at least 3 stages with the top teams competing for the cash prizes and all the glory in stage 4.
