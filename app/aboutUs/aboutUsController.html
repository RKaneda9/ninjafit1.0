Obstacle Training

Are you someone who already competes in Obstacle Course Racing? Have you just recently signed up for your first Spartan or Tough Mudder and are wondering "Where can I go to get prepared?" Our head trainer is a Spartan SGX Certified coach, so all the programming we offer is purpose built to help you get ready for your next race. It doesn't matter whether you're just getting into it or are considered an elite, our curriculum will help you take your game to the next level.

Functional Training

At NinjaFit Gym, our boot camp style functional fitness classes are designed to get you in shape head to toe, burning calories, building strength, increasing your flexibility, and improving your cardiovascular performance, all while having fun in the process. We incorporate free weights, kettlebells, ropes, medicine balls, balance boards, TRX equipment, plyometric/bodyweight exercises, box jumps, squat racks, pull up rigs, plyo boxes, rowers, assault bikes, and more to create a workout experience that will get you fit for LIFE!

Classes

We have coaches/trainers that lead people in a WOD (Workout of the Day) that is built specifically to improve all aspects of ninja, OCR & functional fitness. These classes will integrate a wide range of exercises & obstacles to provide the best overall workout you can get. These classes are run every hour on the hour starting as early as 6am and as late as 8pm (Check hours/schedules for more details).

NinjaFit Kids

We have instructors leading kids through obstacle skill development, and then run them through an actual obstacle course. We've built a progression path for the kids through a "belt system" (just like martial arts) to continually advance themselves and their skills. The NinjaFit Kids program has been a big hit and many kids are well on their way to becoming future American Ninja Warriors!! (Ages 5 & up)
