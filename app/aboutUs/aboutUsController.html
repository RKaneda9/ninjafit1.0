Obstacle Training

Are you interested in... or already mad about... Savage Race, Spartan, Tough Mudder... just to name a few of the many OCR courses ready to be conquered?! Then our Obstacle Course Racing program is for you! Our Spartan designed training classes will prepare any beginner for your first mud run, while challenging the experienced OCR racer focused on increasing strength and endurance, and honing the skills necessary to excel on race day! Come ready for a sweat inducing, fun, challenging workout in a friendly group environment!

Functional Training

At NinjaFit Gym, our boot camp style functional fitness classes are designed to get you in shape head to toe, burning calories, building strength, increasing your flexibility, and improving your cardiovascular performance, all while having fun in the process. We incorporate free weights, kettlebells, ropes, medicine balls, balance boards, TRX equipment, plyometric/bodyweight exercises, box jumps, squat racks, pull up rigs, plyo boxes, rowers, assault bikes, and more to create a workout experience that will get you fit for LIFE!
